About Acadia Park

Acadia Park Equipment Company was founded with a simple goal: to make high-quality outdoor gear accessible to everyone. We believe that spending time outdoors builds stronger communities, healthier lifestyles, and a deeper respect for nature.

Our designs are inspired by national parks, coastal forests, and the people who explore them. Every product is created with durability, comfort, and sustainability in mind.

As part of our mission, we actively support environmental preservation efforts and partner with organizations dedicated to protecting natural spaces for future generations.
